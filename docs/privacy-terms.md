# SimLab Privacy Policy & Terms of Service Outline

This document outlines the privacy governance rules, GDPR/FERPA compliance structures, and terms of service templates for the SimLab platform.

---

## 1. Terms of Service Summary

All users of the Digital Marketing Simulation Laboratory agree to the following conditions:
- **Academic and Instructional Use Only**: SimLab is designed for learning and testing digital marketing skills. Standard accounts may not be used to manage commercial operations.
- **Account Verification**: Users are responsible for maintaining session credential secrecy.
- **Service Availability Limits**: SimLab reserves the right to suspend accounts exceeding simulation and student limit thresholds or violating billing cycles.

---

## 2. Privacy & Data Protection Policy (GDPR & FERPA)

SimLab processes user credentials and student performance telemetry. Our data practices align with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

### Data Retention & Cleanup Policies
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts that remain inactive for more than 180 days are automatically flagged for archival.
- **Student Data Anonymization**: Student name strings are stored securely. When a class is deleted by an instructor, student associations are dissolved, and their daily metric summaries are decoupled from the user ID to preserve privacy.
- **Data Subject Requests**: Users can request complete erasure of their records by contacting academic administrators.

---

## 3. Cookie Consent Implementation Guidelines

The frontend must display a Cookie Consent Banner for first-time visitors:
- **Purpose**: SimLab uses cookies solely for functional authentication sessions (`better-auth.session_token`).
- **Options**: Because we do not use tracking or advertising cookies, the consent banner explicitly details that session cookies are technically required for simulator operations.
