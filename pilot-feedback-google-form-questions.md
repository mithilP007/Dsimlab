# Google Forms Question Template — SimLab Pilot Feedback

Copy-paste standard questions, types, and options for setting up the Google Forms questionnaires.

---

## 1. STUDENT PILOT FEEDBACK (Google Form)

### Q1: Student Name
*   **Type:** Short Answer
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q2: Institutional Email Address
*   **Type:** Short Answer
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q3: Class Join Code (Cohort)
*   **Type:** Short Answer
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q4: Rate the ease of logging in to your SimLab account.
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Extremely Difficult, 5 = Extremely Easy
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q5: Rate the ease of joining your classroom cohort via the invite code.
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Confusing/Broke, 5 = Smooth & Instant
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q6: Rate the usability of the Daily Campaign setup console (SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads).
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Very Clunky, 5 = Highly Intuitive
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q7: How realistic did the daily simulation outcomes feel relative to your inputs?
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Unrealistic/Static, 5 = Dynamic & Real
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q8: Rate the clarity of performance charts and result breakdowns.
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Hard to Understand, 5 = Very Clear
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q9: Did the competitive leaderboard motivate your daily campaign decisions?
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Not at all, 5 = Very Motivating
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q10: Rate the usefulness of daily recommendations/insights for advancing your strategy.
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Useless/Generic, 5 = Actionable & Relevant
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q11: Rate the ease of verification and usefulness of the certification portal.
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Too Complex, 5 = Professional & Seamless
*   **Required:** Yes

### Q12: Did you encounter any technical bugs or validation errors?
*   **Type:** Paragraph
*   **Description:** Please write details (e.g. error message, page path, button clicked).

### Q13: Were there any screens or layouts that you found confusing?
*   **Type:** Paragraph

### Q14: What feature request or improvement would you like to see in the final release?
*   **Type:** Paragraph
